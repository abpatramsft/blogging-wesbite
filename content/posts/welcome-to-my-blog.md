# Welcome to my blog 👋

This is your new **Blog Studio**. Here's what you can do:

- ✍️ Write posts in Markdown on the left
- 👀 Use **Split** or **Preview** to see them rendered live
- 💾 Drafts autosave as you type
- 🚀 Hit **Publish** when you're ready to share

## Markdown works out of the box

You can use `inline code`, [links](https://github.com), lists, and quotes:

> Write freely. Publish boldly.

```js
console.log("Happy blogging!");
```

Posts are saved as Markdown under `content/posts/` in your repo, so you can commit and ship them.
